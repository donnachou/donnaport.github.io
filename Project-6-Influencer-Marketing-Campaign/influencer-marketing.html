Influencer Marketing Campaign – Suprix (Remote)

Problem: Suprix was struggling with low engagement on social media and needed a targeted marketing campaign to reach a broader audience.

Approach: I developed an influencer marketing campaign to increase brand visibility. I selected a group of influencers that matched the brand’s target audience, and collaborated with them to create engaging content that highlighted Suprix’s unique selling points.

Impact: The campaign led to a 50% increase in social media engagement and a 20% rise in customer inquiries, significantly improving brand awareness and consumer interest.

Skills Used: Influencer Marketing, Campaign Management, Social Media Strategy, Brand Visibility
