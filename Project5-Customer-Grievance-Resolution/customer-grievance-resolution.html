Challan Grievances Resolution – Cars24 Internship Project

Problem: Customers were facing issues with challan discrepancies, resulting in negative feedback and a poor user experience.

Approach: I implemented a customer feedback loop to track and resolve grievances related to challans. I worked closely with the support team to gather data and introduced automated systems to address common issues while offering better user support.

Impact: By addressing 95% of grievances and launching an improved FAQ and support flow, we were able to enhance the overall user experience and increase customer satisfaction by 40%.

Skills Used: Customer Support, User Feedback, Issue Resolution, Process Optimization
