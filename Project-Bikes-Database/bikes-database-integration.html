Bike Database Integration – Cars24 Internship Project

Problem: The search functionality for bikes on Cars24 was inaccurate, resulting in poor user experience and increased drop-offs during searches.

Approach: I integrated a database of over 10,000 bikes into the Orbit platform to enhance the search functionality. This involved cleaning and organizing the data, ensuring accuracy, and optimizing filtering capabilities.

Impact: The accuracy of the search improved significantly, leading to a decrease in drop-off rates and an increase in user engagement. This resulted in better conversions and overall user satisfaction.

Skills Used: MySQL, Data Integration, Collaboration with Development Team
