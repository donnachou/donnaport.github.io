Wireframing and CMS Management – Cars24 Internship Project

Problem: Cars24 required a streamlined CMS to better manage content updates and product listings, as well as a robust user interface for product managers.

Approach: I worked on wireframing the CMS interface, ensuring it was user-friendly and intuitive. I created wireframes using tools like Figma and collaborated with the development team to integrate them into the CMS.

Impact: The new CMS streamlined content management, reducing update times by 30%. The wireframes contributed to an enhanced user interface, leading to smoother workflows and more efficient content updates across the platform.

Skills Used: Wireframing, CMS Management, UI/UX Design, Collaboration with Developers
