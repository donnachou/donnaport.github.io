Offline Challan Pilot – Cars24 Internship Project

Problem: Users were facing difficulties paying traffic challans online due to slow processing times, leading to high drop-offs in the payment flow.

Approach: I managed the design and implementation of an offline challan payment flow pilot, where users could pay their fines offline through partner stores. I worked on user research, feature development, and testing to improve the overall user experience during the payment process.

Impact: The pilot resulted in a 25% reduction in drop-off rates, with an 80% increase in successful payments during the trial period. It also paved the way for a full-scale offline payment solution.

Skills Used: Product Design, User Research, Flow Optimization, Payment Integration
